Frequently Asked Questions

Are these seeds legal to purchase?

Yes, our seeds are sold strictly as novelty souvenirs and for genetic preservation purposes. Please check and adhere to your local and state laws regarding cannabis cultivation.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days. Domestic shipping within the US usually takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods. Please contact us directly or proceed to checkout to see the currently available options for your region.

Do you offer a germination guarantee?

Because our seeds are sold as novelty items, we do not provide a germination guarantee. However, we take immense pride in our quality control and storage standards to ensure the highest viability possible.

Can I become a wholesale partner?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated wholesale program for retail shops and commercial facilities. Please visit our Wholesale page and fill out the inquiry form to get started.
